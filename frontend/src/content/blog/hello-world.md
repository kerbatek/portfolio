Things like:

- a 5-hour battle with Linux NIC drivers without internet access
- a power outage that took down DNS for my whole family while I was away from home
- a one-way bottleneck on a full-duplex connection
- an MTU mismatch that made my static HTML site take 12 seconds to load
- an accidental `rm -rf` on the NAS D:

So I decided to write it down.

I'm a Flutter dev making my way into DevOps and cloud. Most of what ends up here will be homelab stuff, cert notes (CCNA, AWS SAA), and whatever interesting things I ran into along the way.

Nothing polished. Just things I think are worth writing down :)
